Learning With Flashcards


This week in babyroom we will be washing the babies and identifying body parts i.e mouth, ears, nose using favourite teddies. We will be using flash cards to develop speech and using stickle brick to build the biggest tower.

Read Post

In toddlers we are exploring different types of music. country, classical etc. We will be dancing and listening to how the musical instruments sound whilst playing in small groups.

Visit Us

Preschoolers continue the preparations for their graduation. In our small world area we have dinosaurs and will be reading our most favourite book ‘Harry and the bucket of dinosaurs’.

We will also be learning about transport and using different vehicles tyres to make prints with paint.

Enjoy the sunshine and have a lovely week.
